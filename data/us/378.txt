bp texas city loses power, residents warned
bp plc's 475,000 barrel per day (bpd) refinery in texas city, texas, lost power at about 10:55 p.m. local time monday (0355 gmt tuesday), frightening local residents who were ordered inside to avoid breathing smoke from the refinery's flares.
