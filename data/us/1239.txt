fbi seeks dna from "unabomber" for tylenol case
the chicago office of the fbi is asking for dna samples from "unabomber" ted kaczynski in connection with an investigation into an unsolved 1982 crime in which seven people died after taking tylenol capsules laced with potassium cyanide, fbi officials said on thursday.
