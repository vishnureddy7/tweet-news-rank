u.s. finds tiny amount of radiation in milk
a trace amount of radioactive iodine, well below levels of public health concerns, has been detected in milk from the state of washington as the u.s. monitors radiation levels amid the nuclear crisis in japan, u.s. regulators said on wednesday.
