imf approves 26 billion euro loan for portugal
the international monetary fund on friday approved a 26 billion euro ($37 billion) loan for portugal to help the country recover from a debilitating sovereign debt crisis, saying it would immediately disburse 6.1 billion euros to ease investor concerns over the euro zone member's debts.
