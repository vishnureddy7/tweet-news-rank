peru run-off to pit humala against fujimori: poll
a datum poll shows left-wing candidate ollanta humala winning the first round of peru's presidential election on sunday with 31.9 percent of the vote, followed by rightist lawmaker keiko fujimori with 22.3 percent, a source with access to the poll said on friday.
