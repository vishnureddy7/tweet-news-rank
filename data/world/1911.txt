saudi arrests 100 shi'ite protesters: rights group
saudi authorities arrested 100 shi'ite protesters during demonstrations in the east of the country last week, a saudi human rights group said on wednesday. hundreds attended protests in and around the region's main shi'ite center, qatif, calling for the release of prisoners and withdrawal of saudi forces from bahrain.
