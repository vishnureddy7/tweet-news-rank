china 2010 census shows 1.3 billion population
china's population grew to 1.34 billion by 2010, according to census figures released on thursday, up 5.9 percent from the 1.27 billion counted in the last census in 2000, and lower than the 1.4 billion population some demographers had projected for the latest tally.
