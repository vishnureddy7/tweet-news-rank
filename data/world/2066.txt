afghan taliban say insufficient evidence bin laden is dead
the afghan taliban said on tuesday they had not seen sufficient evidence yet to convince them that al qaeda leader osama bin laden is dead, their first comment since u.s. officials said the mastermind of the september 11, 2001 attacks was killed in pakistan.
