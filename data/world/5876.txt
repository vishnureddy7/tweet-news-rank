cuba gives chilean 20-year sentence in graft case
a cuban court has sentenced chilean businessman max marambio, once a close friend of fidel castro, to 20 years in prison and a former cuban food industry minister to 15 years after convicting them in a high-profile corruption case, official media reported on thursday.
