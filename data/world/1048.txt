india's congress paid for votes in '08: u.s. cable
a u.s. diplomatic cable obtained by wikileaks says that a political aide told a u.s. embassy official that india's ruling congress party officials paid some members of parliament $2.2 million each to get them to back prime minister manmohan singh's government in a 2008 confidence vote.
