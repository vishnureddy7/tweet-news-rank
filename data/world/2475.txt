yemen deal may be done within week: officials
an agreement brokered by gulf arab states for yemeni president ali abdullah saleh to give up power could be finalized within a week, officials said on tuesday, as yemen struggles to avoid plunging deeper into chaos. an opposition official said the secretary-general of the gulf cooperation council, abdullatif al-zayani, was expected to visit the capital sanaa on wednesday with an invitation to a signing ceremony in riyadh early next week.
