japan pm eyes june cabinet reshuffle: report
japanese prime minister naoto kan is considering reshuffling his cabinet as soon as june, in a bid to reinforce the government's reconstruction efforts after a massive quake and tsunami and to strengthen his standing within the ruling party, kyodo news agency said on wednesday.
