japan voters want coalition to handle crisis: poll
nearly two-thirds of japanese voters believe the ruling democratic party should form a coalition with the main opposition to better handle a prolonged nuclear crisis and recovery effort after a huge earthquake and tsunami, an opinion poll published late on sunday showed.
