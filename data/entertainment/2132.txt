'the book of mormon' tour to open in denver
"the book of mormon" will open its tour in denver, in the home state of two of the show's creators, trey parker and matt stone, the creators of "south park." other broadway hits that have announced tour itineraries recently include "memphis," "the addams family" and "war horse."
