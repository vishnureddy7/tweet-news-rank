kid rock, sheryl crow touring together
kid rock is taking sheryl crow -- his duet partner on the 2002 hit "picture" -- on the road this summer as a special tour guest, with a potential live album to follow. the duo will play 27 shows together starting july 2 in cincinnati and finishing september 3 in houston.
