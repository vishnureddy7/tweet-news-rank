'spider-man' frozen, and actors are relieved
after months of rewrites, new lyrics and choreography, rehearsals fo new material by day and preview performances at night, the cast and crew of "spider-man: turn off the dark" was relieved when the production was "frozen" after 177 performances, meaning no more changes before opening night on june 14.
