playwrights horizons announces 2011-12 season
playwrights horizons on thursday announced five of the six productions in its 2011-12 line-up, including a world premiere from gina gionfriddo (a pulitzer prize finalist for "becky shaw") and directing gigs for sam gold (who staged the theater's current hit "kin") and pam mackinnon (director of its 2010 hit "clybourne park").
