netflix, miramax strike multi-year streaming deal
netflix and miramax on monday unveiled a multi-year agreement, under which netflix streaming members in the u.s. will get to access movies from the miramax film library, including "pulp fiction," "shakespeare in love," "the english patient," "good will hunting," "bad santa," "scream," "spy kids," "the piano" and "kill bill."
