prince william back at work after royal wedding
it was back to the day job for britain's prince william on tuesday, four days after he married long-term girlfriend kate middleton in a royal wedding watched by hundreds of millions of people around the world. the 28-year-old returned to his royal air force (raf) base in anglesey, wales, in time to resume his job as a search and rescue pilot, after a short break at a secret location.
